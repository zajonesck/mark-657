Our Goals

Stand Up For You

At the core of our mission is a commitment to serving our members and putting their needs first. We believe that the strength of our local lies in the collective power of its members. Our approach includes:

  • Transparency

    We will ensure open and honest communication, keeping members informed and involved in all decisions.

  • Advocacy

    We will tirelessly advocate for the rights and interests of our members, both within the union and in the broader community.

  • Engagement

    We will actively engage with our members, seeking their input and feedback to guide our actions and policies.

  • Empowerment

    We will empower our members by providing the resources, training, and opportunities they need to succeed and grow.
