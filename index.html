Hi, I'm Mike Chavira

Ensure Honesty, Promote Innovation, Foster Transparency

I’m a 32-year Teamster and have been with UPS for 33 years, serving one year part-time, 24 years as a package car driver, and the last eight years as a Feeder driver. During this time, I have also taken on the role of Feeder Union Steward.

This November, I am running for President of our local on the Members First Slate in our upcoming election. My goal is to increase transparency, grow our membership, and return power to our most valuable asset: our members. People have asked what sets our slate apart from the current Executive Board. The answer is simple—we are here to serve the members, while they are here to rule.

Our Goals

Stand Up For You

At the core of our mission is a commitment to serving our members and putting their needs first. We believe that the strength of our local lies in the collective power of its members. Our approach includes:

  • Honesty

    We will uphold the highest standards of honesty by providing transparent communication, trustworthy resources, and genuine opportunities to ensure the integrity and success of our members.

  • Innovation

    We will leverage technology to advocate for the rights and interests of our members, both within the union and in the broader community. Additionally, we will actively engage with our members, encouraging them to provide feedback to guide our actions and policies.

  • Transparency

    We will ensure open and honest communication, keeping members informed and involved in all decisions.
